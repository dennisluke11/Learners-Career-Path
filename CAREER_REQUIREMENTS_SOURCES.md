## South Africa (ZA)

### Official Sources

1. **Department of Higher Education and Training (DHET)**
   - Website: https://www.dhet.gov.za
   - Role: Sets national policies for higher education
   - Admission requirements: General guidelines

2. **Universities South Africa (USAf)**
   - Website: https://www.usaf.ac.za
   - Role: Represents all public universities
   - Admission requirements: University-specific requirements

3. **Central Applications Office (CAO)**
   - Website: https://www.cao.ac.za
   - Role: Centralized applications for KwaZulu-Natal universities
   - Admission requirements: Specific requirements per university

4. **Individual Universities** (Primary Source)
   - **University of Cape Town (UCT)**: https://www.uct.ac.za/apply/requirements
   - **University of the Witwatersrand (Wits)**: https://www.wits.ac.za/study-at-wits/undergraduate/entry-requirements
   - **University of Pretoria**: https://www.up.ac.za/study-at-up/article/2799996/undergraduate-admission-requirements
   - **Stellenbosch University**: https://www.sun.ac.za/english/study/apply/undergraduate
   - **University of Johannesburg**: https://www.uj.ac.za/study/undergraduate/entry-requirements
   - **University of KwaZulu-Natal (UKZN)**: https://www.ukzn.ac.za/study-options/undergraduate
   - **North-West University**: https://www.nwu.ac.za/study-options/undergraduate
   - **University of the Free State**: https://www.ufs.ac.za/prospective-students/undergraduate
   - **Rhodes University**: https://www.ru.ac.za/study/admission/undergraduate
   - **Nelson Mandela University**: https://www.mandela.ac.za/Study/Apply/Undergraduate-Applications

### Key Information

- **APS (Admission Point Score)**: Most universities use APS system
- **Subject-specific requirements**: Vary by program
- **Language requirements**: English proficiency (Home Language or First Additional Language)
- **Mathematics vs Mathematical Literacy**: Engineering/Medicine require Mathematics; others may accept Mathematical Literacy

### Example: Medicine (MBChB)

**University of Cape Town (UCT)**:
- Mathematics: 70% (not Mathematical Literacy)
- Physical Sciences: 70%
- Life Sciences: 70%
- English (Home Language): 60% OR English (First Additional Language): 70%
- APS: 35+ (out of 42)

**University of the Witwatersrand (Wits)**:
- Mathematics: 70%
- Physical Sciences: 70%
- Life Sciences: 70%
- English (Home Language): 60% OR English (First Additional Language): 70%
- APS: 35+

**University of Pretoria**:
- Mathematics: 70%
- Physical Sciences: 70%
- Life Sciences: 70%
- English (Home Language): 60% OR English (First Additional Language): 70%
- APS: 35+

## General Verification Process

### Step 1: Identify the Career
- Map career name to actual university programs
- Example: "Doctor" → "MBChB" or "Medicine"

### Step 2: Check Official Sources
1. Start with national regulatory bodies
2. Check individual university websites
3. Verify current year requirements (they change annually)

### Step 3: Document Requirements
- Minimum grades per subject
- APS/points requirements (if applicable)
- Subject combinations
- Language requirements
- Date verified
- Source URL

### Step 4: Update Data
- Update `firestore-data/careers.json`
- Update `src/app/features/career-planning/services/careers-data.helper.ts`
- Add source attribution
- Run verification script
