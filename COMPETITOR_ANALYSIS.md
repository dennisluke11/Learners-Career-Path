# Competitor Analysis: Career Guidance Platforms

## Your Application: Learner's Career Path

**Core Value Proposition:**
- Career guidance platform for African high school students
- Reverse eligibility system: Select desired career → See required improvements
- Real-time grade tracking with instant calculations
- Multi-country support (South Africa, Kenya, Nigeria, Zimbabwe, Ethiopia, Egypt)
- AI-powered study resources (Google Gemini)
- Either/or subject logic (Math/MathLiteracy, English/EnglishFAL)
- Visual progress charts and subject breakdowns
- Career market data (salary ranges, job counts, trends)

## Your Unique Differentiators

### 1. **Either/Or Subject Logic** ⭐
- **Math OR MathLiteracy** - Most apps treat these as separate requirements
- **English (Home Language) OR English (First Additional Language)** - Your logic handles this elegantly
- **Visual representation** - Shows "Mathematics OR Mathematical Literacy" clearly
- **Progress tracking** - Correctly calculates progress for either/or pairs

### 2. **Reverse Eligibility System** ⭐
- Most apps: "What careers can I do with my grades?"
- Your app: "I want to be a Doctor → What do I need to improve?"
- More goal-oriented and actionable

### 3. **Real-Time Grade Tracking** ⭐
- Instant recalculation as user types
- No "Calculate" button needed
- Immediate feedback

### 4. **Multi-Country African Support** ⭐
- South Africa, Kenya, Nigeria, Zimbabwe, Ethiopia, Egypt
- Country-specific subjects, curricula, grade systems
- Localized career requirements

### 5. **AI-Powered Study Resources** ⭐
- Google Gemini AI generates personalized resources
- Country-specific and subject-specific
- Dynamic content generation

### 6. **Visual Progress Breakdown** ⭐
- Subject-by-subject progress bars
- Overall progress tracking
- Clear improvement targets

### 7. **Career Market Data** ⭐
- Salary ranges by level (junior, mid, senior)
- Job counts by platform
- Market trends
- Currency-specific (KES, ZAR, etc.)

## Market Gaps & Opportunities

### 1. **Either/Or Subject Handling**
- **Gap:** Most platforms don't explicitly handle either/or requirements
- **Your Advantage:** You've built sophisticated logic for this
- **Opportunity:** Market this as a key differentiator

### 2. **African Market Focus**
- **Gap:** Most platforms are US/Canada/Europe focused
- **Your Advantage:** Built specifically for African education systems
- **Opportunity:** Expand to more African countries

### 3. **Real-Time Feedback**
- **Gap:** Many platforms require manual calculation or submission
- **Your Advantage:** Instant feedback as user types
- **Opportunity:** Emphasize user experience

### 4. **Reverse Eligibility**
- **Gap:** Most platforms show "what you qualify for"
- **Your Advantage:** Shows "what you need to achieve your goal"
- **Opportunity:** Position as goal-oriented career planning

### 5. **AI-Powered Resources**
- **Gap:** Many platforms have static study resources
- **Your Advantage:** Dynamic, personalized AI-generated content
- **Opportunity:** Expand AI features (career recommendations, study plans)
