## Key Notes

1. **Mathematics vs Mathematical Literacy**:
   - **Required Mathematics** (NOT Mathematical Literacy): Doctor, Engineer, Software Engineer
   - **Mathematics OR Mathematical Literacy**: Lawyer, Nurse, Teacher, Accountant, IT Specialist

2. **English Requirements**:
   - Most programs accept **English Home Language** OR **English First Additional Language**
   - FAL requirements are typically 10% higher than Home Language

3. **APS Requirements**:
   - Competitive programs (Medicine, Engineering): APS 35+
   - Standard programs: APS 26-32
   - Diploma programs: APS 20-22

4. **Subject Levels**:
   - Level 6 = 70-79% (Meritorious Achievement)
   - Level 5 = 60-69% (Substantial Achievement)
   - Level 4 = 50-59% (Adequate Achievement)
   - Level 3 = 40-49% (Moderate Achievement)
