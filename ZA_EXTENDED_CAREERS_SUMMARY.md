## Important Notes

### Mathematics vs Mathematical Literacy
- **Required Mathematics (NOT Mathematical Literacy)**:
  - All Engineering disciplines
  - Computer Science, Data Science
  - Physics, Mathematics
  - Economics

- **Mathematics OR Mathematical Literacy acceptable**:
  - Most Business careers (except Economics)
  - IT specializations (except Computer Science/Data Science)
  - Humanities
  - Most Science careers (except Physics/Mathematics)

### English Requirements
- Most programs accept **English Home Language** OR **English First Additional Language**
- FAL requirements are typically 10% higher than Home Language
- Humanities careers require very strong English (60-70%+)

### APS Requirements
- **Very Competitive** (APS 35+): Medicine, Engineering, Mathematics
- **Competitive** (APS 30-34): Economics, Physics, Computer Science
- **Standard** (APS 26-29): Most Business, IT, Humanities, Science
- **Diploma** (APS 20-22): Diploma programs
