Introduction

Learner's Career Path ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web application at https://learner-s-career-path.web.app (the "Service").

Information We Collect

Important: The information you enter (country, grade level, academic grades, career preferences) is processed locally in your browser and is not stored or saved on our servers. We use this information only to perform calculations and provide you with career recommendations in real-time.

Information You Provide (Processed Locally, Not Stored)

  • Country Selection: Used to provide relevant educational information and calculate eligibility
  • Grade Level: Used to customize the experience and calculations
  • Academic Grades: Used locally to calculate career eligibility - not stored
  • Career Preferences: Used for planning and recommendations - not stored

Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to collect usage statistics (page views, interactions, etc.)
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the Service, features used, and time spent

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Service Functionality: Calculate career eligibility, provide recommendations, show university information
  2. Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  3. Communication: Send push notifications about relevant announcements (if you opt-in)
  4. Analytics: Understand how users interact with the Service and improve user experience

Data Storage and Security

We do not store your personal information or academic grades. All data you enter (country selection, grade level, subject grades, and career preferences) is processed locally in your browser using algorithms to calculate career eligibility and recommendations. This data is never saved to our servers or any database.

We use Google Firebase Firestore only to store:

  • Public educational data (career requirements, university information, country rules)
  • Push notification preferences (if you opt-in, stored anonymously)
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data

We use encryption, Firestore security rules, and rate limiting to protect all stored information. We do not require or store personal identifying information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You can use the Service without creating an account
  • You can clear your browser's local storage to remove stored preferences
  • You can enable or disable push notifications at any time
  • You can opt-out of analytics by disabling cookies in your browser settings

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@learnerscareerpath.com
